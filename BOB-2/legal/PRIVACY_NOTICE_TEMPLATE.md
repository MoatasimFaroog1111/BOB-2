## 1. Scope

This notice explains how [Controller] processes personal data when customers and authorized users access GuardianAI Accountant / BOB-2, related support channels, implementation services and integrations.

## 2. Personal data categories

Depending on the customer's configuration, the service may process:

- account identity and contact data;
- organization, role and authorization data;
- employee, supplier and customer information contained in accounting records;
- invoices, receipts, bank statements and supporting documents;
- ERP connection metadata and audit events;
- IP address, device/browser information and security events;
- support communications;
- optional Telegram or external-LLM processing data only when expressly enabled and authorized.

The service must not be used to upload unnecessary sensitive personal data. Customers are responsible for configuring access and supplying only data needed for the agreed purposes.

## 7. Retention and deletion

Data is retained only for the agreed service, legal, tax, accounting, security and dispute periods. Deletion requests are assessed against statutory retention and legal-hold duties. Where immediate deletion is not lawful, access is restricted and the data is deleted or anonymized after the applicable duty ends.

## 8. Security

Controls include tenant isolation, role-based authorization, MFA, encrypted secrets, TLS, database backups, audit logging, dependency scanning and incident procedures. No service can guarantee absolute security; customers must protect credentials, configure roles and review financial postings.
