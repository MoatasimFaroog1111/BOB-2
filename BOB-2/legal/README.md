# Commercial Legal Pack

These templates are operational starting points for counsel review. They are not legal advice and are not executed agreements.

## Required before selling or processing customer data

1. Identify the contracting legal entity, commercial registration, VAT number and authorized signatory.
2. Decide whether the service is SaaS, customer-hosted, implementation services, or a combination.
3. Complete and obtain legal approval for:
   - Privacy Notice;
   - Data Processing Addendum;
   - SaaS Order Form / commercial terms;
   - Security and Support Schedule;
   - subprocessors and data-location schedule;
   - retention and deletion schedule.
4. Register or complete the applicable private-entity and self-assessment steps on the Saudi National Data Governance Platform where required.
5. Record the controller/processor roles for every data flow.
6. Do not transfer personal data outside the approved locations without the required assessment and contractual safeguards.
7. Keep an incident-notification procedure and customer contact list.
8. Obtain a qualified Saudi lawyer's written approval before changing the status to `LEGAL APPROVED`.
